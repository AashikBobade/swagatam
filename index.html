Exciting news for JEE Students!

IIT Ropar launches various initiatives to integrate them into the IIT system:

Everyday Interaction at 8 pm (Online)

A daily online interaction session of professors and Students at 8 pm with JEE qualified students to tell them about the IIT Culture.

Zoom link
Physical Interaction at 11 am

A daily in-person interaction session at 11 am in the CSE department.
Provides opportunities for face-to-face discussions with the parents to solve their doubts

Location - CSE Department, IIT Ropar
Technical Workshop (Online and Offline)

Focused on the "coaching to campus" and "the missing semester" initiative.
Aims to bridge the gap between academic learning and practical application for the students to have a smooth transition from school to college

Minor in AI across all branches

To give the students a taste of AI, a minor in AI would be provided to the students all over India to help them with the concepts involved in AI.
